About Me

I am a Robotics and AI Engineering student at Airlangga University, and frankly, I am completely hyper fixated on the intersection of hardware and software. I don't just build systems, I am deeply and utterly obsessed with making them think, move, and see. My workspace is basically a 24/7 stream of caffeine, circuit schematics, and terminal windows and obviusly LLM that always open right next to my compiler.

I don't code in isolation. I am a massive believer in leveraging advanced AI as an active engineering collaborator. From prompt-engineering custom dev workflows to treating LLMs as an extension of my own brain, I utilize AI to break through roadblocks and prototype.

My absolute sweet spot is giving physical machines sight. I thrive on plugging cameras into microcontrollers, tweaking computer vision models, and watching the live feed capture reality in real-time. If it involves electronics, 3D mechanical design, and a lens, I will stay up all night making sure it will runs flawlessly no matter what it takes.

Right now, I'm expanding my universe into cloud computing because I think local networks aren't enough, and I want to bring my hardware creations fully online to the rest of the world. I have a massive love for the aesthetic of raw electronics and a brain wired for continuous learning. Let’s connect and build something slightly chaotic > < !.
